Discover the Secret Code

Welcome to Discover the Secret Code Game

There is a secret color code you have to break. You have 12 attempts.

Rules

Click on one out of eight colors in the color picker below the play board. Choose five colors. Colors can be repeated. Once you are happy with the choice of all five colors, press the Check button. Your clues will be shown on the right.

Clues

  • White circle - you guessed a color, but not its position
  • Black circle - you guessed a color, and its position
  • Grey circle - wrong color was chosen

The position of the black, white and grey circles does not correspond to a particular color choice. If you guess the color code in less than 12 attempts, you win. If not, too bad, but you can try again. Enjoy!
